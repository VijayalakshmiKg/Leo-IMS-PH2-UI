Two factor authentication
Step 1: Install Google / Microsoft Authenticator

Download the Authenticator app on your mobile device from the App store or Google Play.

Step 2: Add your account

Open the Authenticator app. Tap the plus button and select ‘Scan a QR code’.

Step 3: Scan the QR Code

Use the Authenticator app to scan the QR code

OR

Alternatively, you may enter the following code to the Authenticator App

ZHAKLAHSAKJHHFAJSKFHAKJHFSAF
Step 4: Enter the 6-Digit code

Enter the 6-digit verification code generated by the Authenticator app

Step 5: Verify and enable

Click ‘Verify’ to complete the setup and secure your account.
